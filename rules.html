Server Rules

Please follow these rules to ensure a great experience for everyone

Chat Rules

  • No spamming or flooding the chat
  • No excessive caps or symbols
  • No advertising other servers
  • Be respectful to all players
  • No hate speech or harassment

Gameplay Rules

  • No hacking or cheating
  • No exploiting bugs
  • No griefing other players
  • No team griefing in games
  • Play fair and have fun!

Account Rules

  • No inappropriate usernames
  • No account sharing
  • No alternate accounts in games
  • Keep your password secure
  • Report suspicious activity

Store & Trading Rules

  • No real money trading
  • No scamming other players
  • No begging for items/ranks
  • Trade only in designated areas
  • Report suspicious trades

Important Note

Breaking these rules may result in temporary or permanent suspension from our services. Our staff team reserves the right to take appropriate action against any violation of these rules.
